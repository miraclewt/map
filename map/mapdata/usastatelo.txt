Low-resolution outlines and names for the 50 United States plus the
District of Columbia, stored in shapefile format with coordinates in 
latitude (Y) and longitude (X) in degrees. Suggested label point
coordinates and average population density for each state in 2000 are 
included also. There are four attribute fields:

                 Name: State name or 'District of Columbia'
             LabelLat: Latitude of label point, in degrees
             LabelLon: Longitude of label point, in degrees
          PopDens2000: Average population density by state 
                       in persons per square mile.

Source
------
Based on data from the CIA World DataBank II and the U.S. Census Bureau site 
"State and County QuickFacts" (http://quickfacts.census.gov/qfd/index.html).
